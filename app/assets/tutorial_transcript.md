Welcome to the donor outreach review screen. Here is the whole workflow in
one minute.

Start on the left. Set your campaign, and set the as-of date to the day your
donor file was exported, not today. Dates like lapsed status are calculated
from it, so this one field keeps every run honest.

Upload your donor file, or press Try the sample file. The system reads each
donor's actual gift history and verifies everything else against it. It
checks that the tiers match the giving, that the totals balance, and that
the dates make sense. Anything wrong is held back and listed under Data
problems, in plain language, with a suggested fix.

The Fix and resubmit tab is where you stay in control. Nothing is corrected
automatically. You approve each fix, re-run the checks, and download the
corrected file for your donor database.

Why all this ceremony? The original version of this tool kept the donor list
inside its own instructions and trusted every label. In our sample file of
fifty donors, four tiers were wrong, and one donor would have been asked for
the wrong amount in every letter. At five thousand donors, that is four
hundred wrong letters, sent with confidence, discovered by donors instead of
by you.

Last, the Letter style tab. Edit the drafted letters the way you like them,
upload your edits, and after the same change shows up three times the system
will suggest adopting it. A person approves it by name, and it can only ever
change the personality of a letter, never a number, never a claim.

Nothing here sends email. You review, you approve, you stay in charge.
